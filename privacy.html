Privacy Policy for NustMart

Last updated: January 17, 2026

Zivxio ("we," "our," or "us") operates the NustMart mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

A. Personal Data
We collect your Email Address (for authentication) and Name (for your profile display).

B. Permissions & User Content
To provide the marketplace features, we request the following permissions:

  • Camera & Storage: Strictly used to allow you to take or upload photos of items you wish to sell.
  • Location: Used to tag the location of the items you are selling.

2. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to handle data and functionality:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication, database, and analytics.
  • ImgBB API: For hosting the images of items you upload.
  • Google AdMob: To display advertisements.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to facilitate the buying and selling process and to prevent fraud. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.
